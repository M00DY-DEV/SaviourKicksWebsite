We have our price ranges but we understand every shoe has a unique history, so for that reason we offer quotes individually per sneaker. Some shoes may not look that bad, but in actuality require extra work compared to a shoe that's really dirty.


This way, extra steps can be taken to make sure your shoes meet your requirements, because not all the time is a "simple clean" enough. This ensures that the client and Saviour Kicks have a mutual understanding on what will be done to the shoes.

The more, the better! We can work out pricing that's fair for both parties considering the necessary services on all shoes.

As a small business, we are currently doing a local meet up system to remain flexible with our availability and in accordance with our clients'. This is beneficial to our client's as it ensures that our clients can have their pick up and/or drop off at a time and place that works for them.
